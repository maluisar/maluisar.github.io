The accelerated pace at which technology is taking over every single industry has created a nostalgic feeling that makes our society want to go back to the old ways; Organic and non-gmo labels are demanded by consumers, retailers take pride in selling local and hand-made items, and people prefer walking.

In this paradoxical environment where simple, clean and efficient coexists with artisanal and environmentaly friendly, a style is shining in the interior design world - Mexican Modernism.

Photo: MyDomain

When we think about Mexican style, what comes to mind is really a fusion of Spanish esthetic with native Mexican tradition that gives life to colorful and cozy spaces filled with elements in natural materials like wood, organic textiles, clay and stones.

With this history of influence in the culture, it's not surprising that Mexico is integrating its neighbor's style preferences to its own, creating a style that appeals to both, the US and Mexico markets.
